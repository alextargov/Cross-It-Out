    How to use

    Object: The object of the game is collect points as possible as you can, while performing your daily tasks.

    Equipment: Each account consist of starting 50 bonus points.

    Setup: Choose name, time, date and priority of the task, then click add task into some of categories.

    Playing the Game: Once the task is choosen your purpose is to finished it before end of the day. If your task is completed before deadline you will win points based on priority(ps: you will be able to receive more points if you finish each task with highest priority). Else these points will be substracted from accounts points.

    Please note: Negative points are possible.

    Playing the game

    1. Choose some of categories on left sider bar and click on "Add" button.

    2. All tasks form

    2.1 Choose name of category.

    2.2 Choose due time.

    2.3 Choose due date.

    2.4 Choose priority,

    then click on "Add task" button.

    3. Choose button "Calendar" if you want to see all tasks for particular day.

    4. Check your current points on "Pts."

    5. Use "Sort" for filtering tasks (alphabetically, due date, etc.)

    6. Use "How to use" button for more information.
